About This Project

This project showcases the integration of technology in education and the collective efforts of students working collaboratively on assignments. It is an illustration of the practical application of theoretical knowledge.

Assignment Details

For this assignment, you will familiarize yourself with GitHub Pages and the process of designing a page for a business website. Review the reading materials and create your own customized business website. Complete the following:

  • Take screenshots of the output for each of the following tasks:
    • After you upload all the code files to your repository.
    • After you get a message in GitHub Pages saying "Your site is published at...".
    • After you customize and publish your website. (Should not have the same content if downloaded from a template)
  • Paste your screenshots into a Word document and provide a response to the following questions:
    • Explain the purpose of your website.
    • Explain the challenges and limitations you faced while creating the website in GitHub Pages.
    • What steps did you take to overcome the challenges and limitations?
